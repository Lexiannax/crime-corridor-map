About this project

All data sourced from San Leandro Police Department official NIBRS reports and OPD CrimeWatch. This is a public-interest project documenting crime trends for residents of San Leandro and Oakland, CA. Population basis: 86,900 (San Leandro). No personally identifiable information is included.
